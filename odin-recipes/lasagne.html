Lasagne

Description

Lasagne can be a very involved recipe, but here's a recipe to make it easy and delicious.

The best thing is that this is an easy to store and freeze meal that can then become an easy weeknigh meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 oz cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 9 lasagne noodles
  • 1/2 cup water
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. Add ground beef to the hot skillet and cook until brown and crumbly. Drain and discard grease. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, mozzarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the Parmesan, dried parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Spread 3/4 cups of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagne noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mix, and 1/4 cup sauce. Repeat with the next 2 layers of noodles. Pour 1/2 cup of water along edges of the dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10 minutes. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
